I have been involved with the Delta Sigma Phi Fraternity since August of 2023. The fraternity is committed to a "Better Men, Better Lives" motto. I currently own a Candy Business! I had created this company on August 14th, 2023. I had wanted to start a candy company back when I was in high school, but lacked the funding to do so. Snow Bite Candy specializes in Freeze Dried (FD) candies, which are made using a Harvest Right Freeze Dryer. Examples of candies that we sell include FD Skittles, FD Salt-water taffy, FD Starbursts, and FD Marshmallows. I am hoping to have a portion of funds be donated to cancer-treatment charities, as I personally know how it has effected friends and family. My hopes is to expand the company with custom packing, a distribution location, and promote cancer-awareness research with higher percentages of donation. You can visit our website at www.snowbitecandy.com
