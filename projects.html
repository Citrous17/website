During my freshman year, I had developed an interest in the TikTok algorithim. With this interest in mind, I had worked on the TikTokAPI which was origionally made by David Teather. The API utilized webscraping techniques with credentials created with a new TikTok account. With this project, I wanted to do a spinoff project where users could predict if a video would do well or not. I have stopped working on this, but may return to it in the future.

In the Spring of 2023, Tiktok had exploded with videos that combined Reddit stories with relaxing video footage, usually Subway Surfers or even clips from Family Guy! With this project, I had created an automated python script which will download the hotted reddit post at that moment, convert it to text-to-speech, and then combine the TTS with an video clip. The resuling video can then easily be upladed to TikTok. The account got 71 followers and 2246 likes before I suspended the project.
