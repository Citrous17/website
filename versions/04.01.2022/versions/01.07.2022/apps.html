Fruitilicious!

This is the Fruitilicious splash page.

This game draws heavy inspiration from Cookie Clicker and idle clickers. Key components is the ability to tap an apple, the main gameplay of the game. An innovative take is the prestiege levels, which are obtained through challenges.

This is the Fruitilicious in-game Market

This is the pure innovation that sets this game apart from other games in this genre. At it core, the game relies on the user to choose wheen to sell their fruit at a price, determined randomley. The market can crash, skyrocket, side-side or in circles, nobody knows!

This is the Fruitilicious upgrades panel

Here, the user can choose what upgrades would suit them the best. The price of upgrades grow exponentially, while their benefits continue linerally. This is common within the genre.

This is the Fruitilicious research panel

In game, you can research bonuses that will auto-click fruit, increase the ammount of fruit obtained with each click, or increase the maximum price for those fruit. There's a seperate research panel for each fruit!
